Lasagna

Description

A homemade lasagna recipe with a made-from-scratch tomato sauce and cheese filling. This recipe uses a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and parmesan instead of the traditional ricotta.

This recipe should be served with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • Meat (1/2 lb ground pork, 1/2 lb ground beef)
  • Onion (1 diced onion)
  • Canned tomatoes (1 can tomato sauce, 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs (2 tbsp parsley, one clove of garlic)
  • Sugar (just a dash)
  • Spices and seasonings (dried basil, dried oregano, salt, black pepper)
  • Noodles (uncooked or oven-ready lasagna noodles)
  • Cheese (cottage cheese, parmesan, and shredded mozzarella)
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
